Bio

I write software. In 2007, I was one of the first employees at Reductive Labs, the makers of Puppet. Afterwards, I worked for several startups in New York City, including Chartbeat.

Working for startups was most common for me until I worked in Tokyo, Japan, where I gained experience at larger companies, including Cookpad and CyberAgent.

Now, I am living near Nashville, TN, where I aspire to make a positive difference.
